Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I purchase an artwork?

Browse our catalog, click on any artwork, and use the "Inquire to Purchase" button. We'll contact you to arrange payment and shipping.

Are all artworks authentic?

Yes! Every piece is carefully curated and verified. We work directly with Georgian artists to ensure authenticity and quality.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Contact us for detailed shipping information.

Can I sell my art on Ornamenti?

Currently, we curate all content. Artist self-registration will be available in Phase 2. Contact us to submit your work for review.
